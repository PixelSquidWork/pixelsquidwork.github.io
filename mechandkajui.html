Mech and Kajui

Coming Soon...

Mech and Kajui is a upcoming game where you play as a robot fighting against a huge monster.
This game is a 2D based beat them up where you control a mech with a variety of moves which the player can experimented. From slashing, dodging and blocking.
Players can master the moves to beat the Kajui.

I have created a variety of protoypes for this game. One of them being a 3D paper pop up to demonstrate one of the ideas for the games mechanics.

Picking up building and using it as weapon against the monsters was a game idea for this game.
Different buildsings had different attributes for attack and speed.
However this mechanic was removed from the player due to going down another path for the game. This concept was still used for one of the Kajui's attacks later.

Originally, the player also had a jump mechanic. This was plan to be used for more dodging attacks and even for some aerial moves.
However after asking the question why the jump was here. I decided to remove it and focusing on close and far combat for the player without adding in air attacks since this was a heavy metal robot designed.
