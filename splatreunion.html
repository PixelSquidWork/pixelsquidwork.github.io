Splat Reunion

From June to August 2022. I ran a community based Splatoon 2 Tournament called Splat Reunion. The final Splatoon 2 UK tournaments series.
I was the lead organizer for online & offline Tournament. The Grand Finals were held at Belong in Sutton.

Splat Reunion Grand Finals - Full Tournament - 50 + Average Livestream Viewers - Broadcasted August 6, 2022.
I was the tournament/event organizer, coordinator, livestream and community manager for this event series.
I collaborated with Belong & other talatend passionate people to make this event become real over 2022s summer.
